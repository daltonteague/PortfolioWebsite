City Circles

Local. Easy.

City Circles is a location-based group chat app for iOS in which you can easily see what events are going on in town and discuss them with other people in the area.

See what's around.

Check out circles around you on the map to see what events or hotspots are being discussed. If you want, you can create your own circle for your own event!

Easily see what's nearby and how much attention it's been getting!

Check out a snap preview of each event!

Chat about events with other users!

Create Your Profile

You can create your own profile and interact with other users. You can add those you meet in circles as friends and message them directly, invite them to circles, or report them for poor behavior.

City Circles

City Circles uses Firebase for data storage. For more, check out the in-development project on Github!
