Spacer Sprint

Endless Fun.

Spacer Sprint is an endless runner on the iOS App Store created in Unity and scripted in C#.

Dynamic Gameplay!

Try to beat your farthest run while dodging bullets from enemies, collecting coins for upgrades, and jetpack fuel to increase your mobility.

Unlock Characters.

Use coins you collect from your runs to unlock new character skins, some of which come with unique animations!

Take it Retro.

Spacer Sprint also includes a retro mode if you really want that old school feel.
